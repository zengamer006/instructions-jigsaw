How to Play the Jigsaw Puzzle Game

Getting Started

  1. When you open the game, you'll see a scrambled image divided into pieces.
  2. Your goal is to reconstruct the original image by arranging the pieces correctly.

Playing the Game

  1. Click and drag puzzle pieces to move them around the board.
  2. Choose the correct option for the pop up question
  3. Connect matching pieces by placing them next to each other.
  4. Continue until all pieces are correctly placed and the image is complete.

Tips for Success

  • Start with the edge pieces to create a frame.
  • Group similar colors or patterns together.
  • Pay attention to the shapes of the piece connectors.
